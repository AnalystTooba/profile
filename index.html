CV

Tooba Iftikhar


As a gold medalist graduate in City and Regional Planning from Pakistan, I bring over four years of academic distinction and two years of impactful professional and research experience in urban planning, geospatial analysis, and sustainable land use strategies. My work focuses on designing climate-resilient and people-centric cities, using advanced data modeling, remote sensing, and urban scenario development. I leverage technology to inform spatial decision-making and sustainable urban design.
My research and insights have been showcased at national and international platforms, including presentations on climate-resilient urban planning and participation in the National Leadership Summit and Youth-Pivot of National Integration Conference. I have worked across government and private organizations, engaging in policy development, master planning, and urban transformation initiatives. My research has directly informed planning decisions, with government bodies such as the Lahore Development Authority integrating my work into their 2024 Commercialization Policy and related urban strategies.
