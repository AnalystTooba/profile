CV

Tooba Iftikhar


As a gold medalist graduate in City and Regional Planning from Pakistan, I bring over four years of academic distinction and two years of impactful professional and research experience in urban planning, geospatial analysis, and sustainable land use strategies. My work focuses on designing climate-resilient and people-centric cities, underpinned by advanced data modeling, remote sensing, and urban scenario development.
I leverage technology to inform spatial decision-making and sustainable urban design. My research and insights have been showcased at national and international platforms, including presentations on climate-resilient urban planning and participation in the National Leadership Summit and Youth-Pivot of National Integration Conference.
Professionally, I have contributed to Lahore’s 2024 Commercialization Policy at the Lahore Development Authority, spearheaded master planning optimization at NESPAK, and collaborated with Sheher Saaz Pvt Ltd on urban transformation projects.
